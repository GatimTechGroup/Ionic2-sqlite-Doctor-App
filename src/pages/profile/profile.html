Dr Andrew See

OPHTHALMOLOGY

Andrew is a specialist radiation oncologist who has expertise in the management of urological, breast, gastrointestinal, skin and general oncology and is a credentialed prostate brachytherapist. He is trained in stereotactic radiosurgery locally and internationally with a number of research initiatives exploring the merits of this advanced radiation technology in his role as the Head of Clinical Research.
